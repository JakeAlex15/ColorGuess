Welcome to:
Guess The Color!!

Instructions --

This is a simple color guessing game created by jake alexander.
There are 10 different colors built into this game,

they are --
blue, cyan, gold, gray, green,
magenta, orange, red, white, and yellow.

To begin the game, click the 'start' button and type in your guess.
from there, the computer will let you know if you guessed the correct color.
you have unlimited guesses and you'll be given the final amount of attempts at the end!

have fun!! :D
